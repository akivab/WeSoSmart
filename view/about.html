About

Welcome to WeSoSmart, a site built to help Columbia students trade textbooks more easily.

This is a place to look for classes, enroll in those you are interested in, and find those books required for your classes.

To get started, you don't even need an account. Simply go to find books or list your old ones.

To get the full experience out of WeSoSmart, sign up for an account. With this, you can save courses you're interested in, message classmates with questions, leave comments on courses, and list books for others to see.

If you sign up, you can list books as "wanted" or "had" and you will receive updates when they appear. Also, if you cannot find your book, you can go to your course and add the ISBN (by clicking "Add Book"). When an ISBN is registered, it will be seen by everybody from there on in. Finally, all books listed are taken directly from courseworks, so there is much less work to be done by you.
